Who am I?

I’m Hermit Narita, M. Eng. candidate, B. Eng. in Air Transport.

Academically, I currently conduct research on the application of operations research (OR) and intelligent optimization algorithms in the aviation industry and have published several papers in SCI and Ei journals. I also engage in works related to visual design, publishing, and media industries. Culturally, I belong to Sinosphere (漢字文化圈), and I’m keen on studying CJKV unified ideographs and their corresponding languages. In daily life, I’m an aircraft and cityscape photographer, diecast aircraft model collector, writer, calligrapher, cyclist, and radio amateur.

What do I focus on?

My research interest and the major contents of my blog include but are not limited to:

  • Civil aviation
    • Airport planning, design, operations, and management
    • Air traffic control and flow management
    • Civil aviation economics management

    • Greener aviation industry and its sustainable development
  • OR & Intelligent optimization algorithms
